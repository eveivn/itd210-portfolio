About Us

What We Do

Studio Citrus is a collaborative creative project founded by five friends who share a passion for storytelling, and digital worldbuilding. What began as a small group of people meeting in a high school art class has grown into a space where imagination, creativity, and teamwork come together to bring new ideas to life.

Why We Do It

Our goal is to create immersive virtual worlds filled with engaging narratives. Through our projects, we strive to build experiences that spark curiosity, encourage creativity, and invite others to explore storytelling alongside us.

At Studio Citrus, we believe that great ideas grow through collaboration. By combining our different skills and perspectives, we aim to create stories and environments that inspire others to imagine, create, and contribute to worlds beyond their own.
